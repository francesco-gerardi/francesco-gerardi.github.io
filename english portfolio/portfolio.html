the tower of hanoi

The Tower of Hanoi is a classic puzzle involving three rungs and a series of differently sized discs. The goal is to move all the discs from the starting peg to the destination peg, one at a time, respecting the rule that a larger disc cannot be placed on top of a smaller one. It's a game that requires strategy and planning, and can become increasingly complex as the number of discs increases.
